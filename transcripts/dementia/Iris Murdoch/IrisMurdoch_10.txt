Well, I was born in Dublin and my parents are a Protestant Irish. And my father decided for reasons which I've never entirely been able to sort out. Although I can guess that I suppose that some of them decided to leave Ireland. And I was one, I was removed therefore to London, where he went into civil service. So I grew up in London. Although I went to Ireland for holidays and I feel extremely Irish and I feel very involved in Ireland and in what's happening in Ireland now. But I grew up in London and I went to boarding school and then I went on to Oxford.
