Interesting question to him. In a political sense, I mean, thinking back all the way, I'm not sure I was aware of it at the time. My mother was the more liberal of my parents. I mean, my dad was a conservative guy who had kind of made it in his own. Both of my folks had been born abroad and came over and he was a wonderful guy and a great father and a great doctor and a very human person, but very conservative politically. My mother was a little more liberal, a little more progressive. And I think in a political sense that probably rubbed off on me. Not only that, but her side of the family was much more political. I can remember political debates around the Sunday dinner table when we'd visit with her side of the family that were pretty loud and pretty wild. And that usually didn't happen.
