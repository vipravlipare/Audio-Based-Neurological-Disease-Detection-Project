Prior to my fiber work, I was working on microwave systems. First of all, on the 6 GHz point-to-point microwave link. It's pure development work of a product. And then when I went to STL, that's a research lab, I decided that that's a very wonderful time to join in the sort of new generations of wideband transmission medium. That was first of all the circular microwave millimetric waveguide. So I was working at the starting point of 1960 on circular waveguide and its related activities.
